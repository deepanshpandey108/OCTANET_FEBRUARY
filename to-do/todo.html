To-Do List

A to-do list can be used for various purposes to help individuals organize their tasks and manage their time effectively. Here are some common uses:

Task Management: Keep track of daily tasks, errands, and chores to ensure nothing is forgotten or overlooked.
Project Planning: Break down larger projects into smaller, actionable tasks, and monitor progress towards completion.
Prioritization: Assign priorities to tasks to focus on the most important ones first and allocate time and resources accordingly.
Time Management: Schedule tasks based on deadlines and available time slots, helping to balance workload and avoid procrastination.
Goal Setting: Set short-term and long-term goals, and use the to-do list to track progress towards achieving them.
